A free survival horror game — right in your browser

The Backrooms is a first-person survival horror game that runs entirely in your web browser. There is nothing to download, nothing to install and no account to create: press play and you are inside the endless yellow halls. Free, instant, on any modern computer.

An infinite maze

Every run is procedurally generated — mono-yellow office halls, pitch-black pipe tunnels, the flooded poolrooms. No two descents are ever the same.

You are being hunted

Four entities stalk the levels. They follow out of sight, freeze when you spot them, slip behind corners — and strike the moment you look away.

Scavenge & survive

Find weapons, a flashlight and ammunition. Manage your thirst, drink from rusty taps, and decide when to fight and when to run.

Sound is everything

Every noise is synthesized live: the fluorescent hum, your heartbeat, and the whispers that mean something is close. Positional 3D audio — trust your ears.
